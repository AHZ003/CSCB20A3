Welcome to CSCB20

Course Description:

A practical introduction to databases and Web app development. Learn database terminology, querying, and designing relational models. Explore web application development and how they integrate with databases.

Prerequisite

Some experience with Python, Java, or C.

Exclusion

This course may not be taken after - or concurrently with - any C- or D-level CSC course.
